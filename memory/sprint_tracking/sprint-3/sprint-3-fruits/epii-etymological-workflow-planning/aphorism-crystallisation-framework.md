The A-FOUR-ISM Recognition
APHORISM from Greek:

ἀφορισμός (aphorismos) = "definition, delimitation, marking off"
From ἀφορίζω (aphorizō) = "to define, mark off, determine"
ἀπό (apo) "from, away" + ὁρίζω (horizō) "to bound, limit"
From ὅρος (horos) = "boundary, landmark, horizon"

Key Clusters from Our Explorations
Let me identify the major etymological territories we've mapped:

PIE *sekw- (to cut) → Latin signum → signare → significare → assignare → sufficere → satis

PIE *sent- (to go, head for) → sentire → sent → scent → scire → sentiment → sediment

3. The CON-/SAM- Recognition (With-ness as Constitutional)
Latin con- (with, together) ≈ Sanskrit sam- (together, complete) from PIE *sem-/*som-

Core insight: Knowing is always knowing-with; consciousness structurally requires the other.

4. The SAT/SANTA/SAMAVESA Trajectory (Being to Spontaneous Grace)
Sanskrit sat (being) → samvit (consciousness) → samata (equality) → samadhi (effort) → samavesa (grace) → santa (peace)

Core insight: What you seek through effort arrives through grace once you recognize it was always present.

5. The SIGHT/SITE Doubling (Seeing and Location)
PIE *sekw- (to see, follow) → sight (act of seeing) vs Latin situs (position, placement)

Core insight: Every seeing happens from somewhere; the site structures what comes into sight.

6. The SOURCE/SCOURED Chiasm (Origin and Excavation)
Latin surgere (to rise from below) vs ex-curare (to clean out thoroughly)

Core insight: The source is found by scouring; the emptied space IS where origins spring.

7. The AUTH → HARM → HARMONIZE Sequence (Authorization Through Wound)
Latin augere (to increase) → augur (diviner) → Germanic harm (wound) → Greek harmonia (fitting together)

Core insight: Authorization initiates wounding that harmony transforms without eliminating.

8. The PRATYABHIJNA/EPILOGOS Isomorphism (Recognition Not Attainment)
Sanskrit prati-abhi-jna (re-cognition) ≈ Greek epi-logos (meta-gathering)
